Transaction Tracking System

Log every sale and purchase with a few taps. No spreadsheets, no paper records—just a clean digital log of all your farm's money in and out.

The system automatically calculates which crops make you the most money, your exact costs per plant, and seasonal trends—so you know what’s working without doing the math.

+ Calculates true cost per plant including seeds, soil, water, labor

Intelligent Alert System

Get notified when a crop is underperforming, costs spike, or demand is high. It tells you what to plant more of and what to avoid, like a 24/7 farm advisor in your pocket.

+ Gets smarter over time — learns your farm’s patterns

Why Choose GreenSense?

Choose GreenSense because it is the only platform built from the ground up for small-scale greenhouse farmers like you. With GreenSense, you gain a dedicated partner that works 24/7 to give you financial clarity and confidence. It saves you time, reduces waste, and puts data-driven decision-making at your fingertips—no technical expertise required. Ultimately, we empower you to farm smarter, not harder, so you can increase your profits and grow your business with peace of mind.
